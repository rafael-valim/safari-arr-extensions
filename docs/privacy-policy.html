Privacy Policy

Radarr Adder & Sonarr Adder Safari Extensions

Last updated: February 2025

Overview

Radarr Adder and Sonarr Adder are Safari extensions that help you add movies and TV shows to your personal Radarr and Sonarr servers. Both extensions are designed with privacy as a core principle.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No cookies or fingerprinting
  • No user accounts or registration
  • No data sent to any third-party servers
  • No advertising

How the Extensions Work

The extensions communicate exclusively between your Safari browser and your own self-hosted Radarr or Sonarr server. All data — including your server URL, API key, and media metadata — stays entirely on your device and your personal server.

  • Server URL & API key — stored locally in your browser's extension storage (chrome.storage.local). Never transmitted anywhere except to your own server.
  • Website content — the extensions read movie/TV show titles and identifiers from supported websites (IMDB, Rotten Tomatoes, TMDb, Letterboxd, TheTVDB) solely to look up media on your Radarr/Sonarr server. This data is not stored or transmitted to any third party.

Permissions

The extensions request permission to access supported movie and TV websites only to read media information and display the "Add to Radarr/Sonarr" button. No data from these websites is collected or stored beyond the current browsing session.

Changes to This Policy
